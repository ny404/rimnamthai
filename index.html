About Us

Hub Thai restaurant is New York’s latest place to savor the exquisite and mouth watering traditional dishes from Thailand. We are situated at 50 Avenue A, and only yards away from Tomkins Square Park.

The restaurant has an air of a French Café/English bistro. With friendly, attentive staffs and delightful aroma from the kitchen, Hub Thai is all about delivering a unique Thai ambiance.

Our chef is proud to be able to give New Yorkers the chance to taste the delicious mixture of fresh food with herbs and spices that make Thai food the tastiest food in the world.

Growing up in the family catering business, which has been cooking traditional Thai food for generations in the Chumpon region of Thailand, our chef has gained the culinary expertise to be able to prepare and cook Thai food at it’s best.

All customers will be given a warm and friendly welcome. Whether they are going to enjoy the delicious Thai meal experience, or having a quiet drink at the bar, or waiting for a meal to take home, Hub Thai is always available for our customers.
